Introduction

Welcome to our 1st edition of Gurans Cup! Here we bring together the best teams to compete in an exciting environment. Join us as we celebrate sportsmanship and teamwork.

Rules and Regulations

**Subjected to Change**

  • All players must wear shin guards or will not be allowed to play.
  • All players must wear jerseys/shirts during play.
  • Players wearing protective casts must receive written approval by the Tournament director.
  • No jewelry allowed except medical bracelets.
  • Duration of the game - 20 minute halves for all game.
  • Substitutions can be made on the fly.
  • Unlimited substitutions allowed.
  • Balls crossing the touch-line on the ground or in the air will be put in play via kick-in. Defending players must provide a 5 yard distance from the point of occurrence.
  • No slide tackling.
  • Free Kicks- defending players may set up a wall 7 yards from the point of infraction.
  • Referees- There will be no abuse of the referees tolerate, verbal or otherwise. Any indication of such behavior by a coach or parent will result in immediate removal from the tournament site. No verbal abuse of any player, coach or referee will be tolerated.
  • Kick-offs may be kicked in any direction.
  • Goals can be scored from anywhere on the field.
  • Ball crossing the sideline will be put in play via kick-in. Defending players must maintain a 5-yard distance.
  • Goals can be scored from any point on the field.
  • Hand balls result in penalty kicks at the referee's discretion.

The following tiebreakers will decide who the winner is:

  1. Points
  2. Head-to-head
  3. Goal Differential
  4. Goals against
  5. Goal scored
  6. Shoot-out
  7. When there are 3 teams with the same amount of points (3-way tie) the goal differential, goals scored or goals against will decide who wins.
