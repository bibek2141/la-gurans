Rules and Regulations

Player Registration:
  • Every Individual player must show a government issued ID for verification and sign a waiver form on the day or before the tournament.
  • No Outside Food or Alcohol: Strictly prohibited inside the complex.
  • Teams are responsible for disposing of trash in designated areas.
Game Format:
  • First round follows a points-based league format.
  • League and quarter-final games: 25 min per half. Semi-finals and finals: 30 min per half. Tied points/goal difference will be settled by 5 min extra time in Quarter Finals, Semi Finals and Finals.
  • Teams more than 10 minutes late will forfeit (2-0 score).
Team Setup:
  • Regular bracket: 12 players. Seven on-field, unlimited substitutions.
  • 35+ bracket: 12 players. Seven on-field, unlimited substitutions.
Player IDs:
  • All players must be on the roster to play. For the 35+ category, government-issued ID and a physical passport are required for secondary verification if asked by the organizers. (DOB year should be before January 31st, 1990)*
Equipment:
  • Full team uniforms, proper footwear, and shin guards are mandatory. No jewelry or metal-studded cleats. The team that registered first chooses their jersey if there is a color clash. Pennies will be provided by the organizers if no team has a secondary Jersey.
Eligibility:
  • No foreign players allowed. If questioned, the respective player’s community verification is required.
  • One player can only play for one team under the same category.
  • For the 35+ category, as long as the BIRTH YEAR is January 31st 1990 or before, you are eligible to play.
Punctuality:
  • Teams must arrive 30 minutes early.
Conduct:
  • Officials’ decisions are final. Fights, fake IDs, or inappropriate behavior will lead to disqualification. No vulgar language allowed.
Direct and Indirect Kicks:
  • When the ball goes out of play along the sidelines, the game is restarted with a kick-in, not a throw-in.
  • A goal can be scored directly from any kick off except Goal Kick.
Goalkeeper Restrictions:
  • A direct goal from the goalkeeper is not allowed meaning the ball must touch another player first.
Tackling:
  • Any physical challenges will be deemed a foul, and the referee will penalize accordingly.
  • Slide tackling is only permitted to reach a cross or to keep a ball in play.
Substitutes:
  • Unlimited substitutions are allowed as long as the referee is made aware of the changes.
  • Substitutes must enter and leave the field of play at the referees discretion.
Referee Decisions:
  • The referee’s decision is FINAL on all matters during the game, including fouls, cards, and other in-game situations.
FIFA Regulations:
  • For all other rules the laws of FIFA will apply.
Final Decisions:
  • Organizers and management reserve the right to make necessary changes.
Note : 2 player/s changes or modifications to the team roster will be allowed after tie sheet draw and before January 17th 5PM PST, 2025.
